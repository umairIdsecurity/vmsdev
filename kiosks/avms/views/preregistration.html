Preregistration for visitor identification card (VIC)

To proceed with this registration you must:

  1. Have a valid Driver Licence or Passport with you
  2. Have the name and contact email or mobile number of your escorting ASIC Sponsor
  3. Give details of your reason for visiting, including company contact information
  4. Approve the declarations below by checking the boxes

Important information regarding the Visitor Identification Card (VIC)

Please read this information carefully and complete all the sections. Failure to do may result in processing delays.

PRIVACY NOTICE - What does Perth Airport do with you personal information?

Information you provide on the VIC application includes personal information, the collection, use and disclosure of which is governed by the Privacy Act 1988 (Commonwealth).

Perth Airport uses this information internally for the purposes of its functions under the Aviation Transport Security Art 2004 and Aviation Transport Security Regulations 2005

For example, Perth Airport will use this information to process VIC application, to monitor compliance with 28 days limit and to keep a register of every VIC issued

Perth Airport may disclose this information to third parties. For example, personal information will be shared with our Authorised Agents (as approve in our Transport Security Program) who issue VICs.
